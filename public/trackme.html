About Track Me

Track Me is a voluntary feature that lets you share your live location with SafeNex authorities for your safety.

• Location is only shared while the toggle is ON.
• You can stop at any time from this screen.
• Tracking continues even if you close the app or browser.
• Authorities are notified immediately when you start and stop.
• If you enter a danger zone, your position is flagged automatically.

iOS Safari: Keep SafeNex open in your browser. You can lock your screen and tracking will continue while the tab is active.
